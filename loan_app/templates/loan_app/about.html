About Our Company

Steller Finance

Steller Finance (Steller Investment & Finance Solution LTD) is a UK-based credit introducer committed to delivering responsible, well-structured finance solutions to individuals and businesses. We exist to simplify borrowing: translating complex market choices into clear, practical options that suit each client’s circumstances. Our role is to listen, assess and introduce applicants to appropriate lenders, supporting them through the application process while promoting transparency, affordability and long-term financial health.

We recognise that every borrower’s situation is different. Whether you are seeking short-term working capital for your business, funding for home improvements, consolidation of existing debts, education finance, or credit to meet unexpected costs, we tailor our approach to your objectives and constraints. Our advisers prioritise a full understanding of your financial position — income, liabilities, expenditure and future plans — so that any introduction we make is relevant and proportionate.

At Steller Finance we emphasise clarity at every stage. We explain the terms and costs associated with different products, outline the implications of secured versus unsecured borrowing and highlight alternatives where applicable. We also stress the importance of affordability assessments: loans should be manageable within the context of your budget and not undermine essential living costs or business stability.

Our service model is built on three core commitments. First, professional guidance: our team combines practical market knowledge with careful application management to obtain accurate lender feedback quickly. Second, impartiality: while we may receive commissions from lenders for introductions, our recommendations are driven by the suitability of solutions for your circumstances, not by any single lender’s commercial imperative. Third, discreet and secure handling of your data: we operate in line with UK data protection requirements and apply robust security and AML checks when processing applications.

Working with Steller Finance does not mean we lend directly — the decision to provide credit rests with the lender. We nevertheless add value by preparing comprehensive applications, advising on documentation, liaising with lenders on your behalf and clarifying offers so you can compare effectively. Where lenders require regulated activity or specific checks, those tasks are carried out by the lender or an authorised partner.

Tailored introductions to suitable, regulated lenders.

Clear guidance on costs, terms and affordability.

Ongoing support from enquiry to funding and beyond.

Who We Are

Loan Provider for your Better Future

At Steller Investment and Finance Solutions Ltd, we understand that securing a loan can feel like a daunting task. Whether you are a first-time borrower or an experienced investor, the loan landscape can be complex and filled with potential obstacles. To address these challenges, we have assembled a team of skilled financial experts who are passionate about helping you discover the right loan solutions. With years of experience in the financial industry, our team possesses the knowledge and expertise needed to guide you through each step of the borrowing process.

Steller Finance is a client-focused credit introducer operating across the United Kingdom. Our mission is to provide borrowers—both individuals and businesses—with considered access to finance by matching needs with lenders who can deliver responsible, affordable solutions. We know that borrowing decisions are significant: they affect immediate plans and long-term financial wellbeing. That is why we combine careful assessment, candid advice and hands-on application support to help clients make informed, sustainable choices.

We were founded on the belief that access to finance should be straightforward and fair. Our team brings together advisers with diverse experience in banking, consumer lending, commercial credit and compliance. That diversity enables us to serve a broad range of clients: entrepreneurs seeking working capital, homeowners financing improvements, graduates requiring education funding, families managing unforeseen costs and individuals consolidating debt. Regardless of the requirement, our starting point is always the same — listen first, then design a pathway that balances need, risk and affordability.

Our approach is practical and process-driven. At the initial stage we collect a clear, accurate snapshot of your financial position and the objective for borrowing. This includes income, outgoings, existing liabilities and any supporting documentation. With that information we construct an assessment that highlights the kinds of products and lenders most likely to offer suitable terms. Where lenders use specific credit criteria or require guarantors or security, we explain those implications candidly so there are no surprises.

Steller Finance values transparency. When we present options, we summarise the total cost of credit, APR, repayment schedule, likely charges and any potential early repayment penalties. Our advisers will discuss whether a secured product is required and the consequences that security imposes. We also flag alternatives — such as staged funding, invoice finance or negotiation of supplier terms — when these can achieve the client’s goal with lower overall cost or risk.

Compliance and responsible lending are central to our operations. As a credit introducer we operate in accordance with UK regulatory expectations for fair treatment of customers, transparent financial promotions and appropriate handling of personal data. We carry out identity verification and AML screening where required, and we work with FCA-authorised lenders for activities that require regulated status. Our privacy and data-handling practices are designed to safeguard client information and comply with the Data Protection Act 2018.

We put client welfare at the heart of our recommendations. That means assessing the sustainability of repayments, avoiding solutions that create immediate relief at the expense of long-term detriment, and signposting to independent debt advice where necessary. If a particular application is unlikely to achieve the desired outcome, we will explain why and propose practical alternatives, rather than pushing unsuitable credit simply to complete a transaction.

Communication is another pillar of our service. We aim to keep clients informed at every stage — from initial eligibility feedback to lender responses and documentation requirements. Our advisers handle liaison with lenders, follow up on outstanding items and clarify any offer terms to ensure you can compare and decide confidently. We also provide post-funding support: answering queries about repayment schedules, discussing refinancing options and assisting if financial circumstances change.

Steller Finance prides itself on building long-term relationships. Many clients return to us as their circumstances evolve because they value a trusted partner who understands their history and can rapidly identify relevant options. For business clients, we seek to act as a consistent finance advisor capable of supporting growth cycles, seasonal needs and occasional short-term liquidity requirements. For personal clients, we aim to be a reliable source of clear, impartial guidance when life requires financial decisions.

In short, Steller Finance exists to make access to appropriate credit simpler, fairer and more transparent. We do not provide credit ourselves; instead, we apply market knowledge, careful assessment and diligent application management to introduce you to lenders that may help you achieve your financial objectives. If you want a considered, responsible approach to borrowing with a focus on long-term outcomes rather than short-term sales, our team is ready to help.

To learn more or to discuss your requirements, contact Steller Finance . We will be pleased to explain our process and explore options that suit your needs.
